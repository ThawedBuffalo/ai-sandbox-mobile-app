Phase 1 UX Design

Dental Procedure Cost Estimator

A task-first, responsive estimator experience derived from the PRD: searchable procedure selection, coverage-tier comparison, a fast in-place result, and clear legal context so members understand this is an estimate, not adjudication.

Primary User Dental plan member
Core Constraint No PII, synthetic data only
Performance Goal P95 under 300ms

Design Intent

  • Keep the estimator on one page to reduce friction and preserve speed.
  • Show the member amount first, then the plan-paid breakdown.
  • Keep the disclaimer visible in every success state.
  • Treat unsupported procedures and technical failures as guided recovery states.
